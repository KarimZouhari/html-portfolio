My top 5 best animes to watch - Karim Zouhari

Number #5

Jujutsu Kaisen

This anime has really good animation, fights and the plot is very good.

Number #4

Black Clover

The animation is mid tier, however, the history is really good and the writing is really well thought out.

Number #3

Naruto

Contains the best fights out of all of them for sure and the history is really good, the animation is bad at first but it gets better with time.

Number #2

One Piece

The story building carries this anime. The fights are really good and the animation is very good but the pace is horrible.

Number #1

Attack on Titan

Best story of all animes in my opinion, the fights are very good but short the animation is very good from the beginning and the writing is peak.
